Consumer Sign-Up Surface

Kumbu Connect Community Feedback Opt-In Instructions

This page shows the exact public keyword-based SMS sign-up instruction a consumer sees before joining the Kumbu Connect Community Feedback program on toll-free number (833) 632-7146.

Sample CBO Keyword: KIWA Opt-In Type: Consumer-Initiated SMS Program: Community Feedback SMS
This is a public sign-up instruction surface. The consumer must read this instruction and send the keyword from their own phone before any follow-up messages begin.

Join Kumbu Connect Community Feedback

Consumers receive a keyword from a participating CBO. In this example, the participating keyword is KIWA.

Text KIWA to (833) 632-7146 By texting KIWA, you agree to receive SMS messages from Kumbu Connect about community feedback surveys, follow-up questions, and periodic check-ins related to that CBO. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

KIWA is a sample participating CBO keyword used on this proof page. In production, each participating CBO uses its own assigned keyword.

What The Consumer Does

1
Receive the keyword from the participating CBO.

The keyword is shared by staff, a flyer, a poster, or a QR-linked instruction page.

2
Text the keyword from the consumer's own mobile phone.

In this example, the consumer sends KIWA to (833) 632-7146.

3
Receive the first community feedback message.

The program begins only after the consumer has taken that affirmative SMS opt-in action.

What Messages The Consumer Can Expect

Program Purpose

Community feedback surveys, follow-up questions, and optional future check-ins related to the participating CBO.

Frequency

Message frequency varies. A typical feedback session contains 2 to 5 messages.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to opt out. The program also honors END, QUIT, CANCEL, and UNSUBSCRIBE.

Help

Reply HELP for enrollment instructions or contact john@kijenzi.com.

Representative First Message

First Automated Message After Opt-In

Thank you for joining KIWA feedback. Reply with a number from 1 to 10 to rate how KIWA has helped your community.

Why This Counts As Consent

  • The consumer must send the keyword from their own phone.
  • The sign-up instruction explicitly says the user is agreeing to receive SMS messages.
  • The instruction states what the messages are about before the consumer opts in.
  • The instruction clearly states STOP and HELP keywords.
  • Consent is not bundled into a privacy policy, contract, or purchase flow.
